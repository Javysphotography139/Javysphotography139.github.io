About Me

Javy’s Photography by Rafael Javier Jr.


Rafael is a freelance photographer, who resides in Westchester, NY. After retiring from the Marine Corps, Rafael became an ENL teacher in the Bronx. While working with students, he found an interest in art, specifically drawing, painting, and photography. To pursue this passion, Rafael attended The New School, where he learned how to shoot with a professional camera. He has a unique style and vision that is captured through his work. Rafael approaches each session with professionalism and his love for photography.


Thank you for your time. I look forward to creating lifelong memories for you and your family!
