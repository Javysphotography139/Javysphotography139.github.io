About Me

Javy's Photography - Rafael Javier Jr

Rafael is a freelance photographer residing in Westchester, NY. After retiring from the Marine Corps, Rafael became an ENL teacher in the Bronx. While working with students, he discovered an interest in art, specifically drawing, painting, and photography. To pursue this passion, Rafael attended The New School, where he honed his photography skills.

Rafael approaches each session with professionalism and a love for photography, capturing unique moments through his lens.

Thank you for your time. I look forward to creating lifelong memories for you and your family!
